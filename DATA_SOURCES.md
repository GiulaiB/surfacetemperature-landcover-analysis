CHELSA data:
	WGS84 horizontal datum, with the horizontal coordinates expressed in decimal degrees.
	Temperature - CHELSA bio 01 = Mean annual temperature calculated as the average of mean monthly temperatures over the year
	Download: https://os.zhdk.cloud.switch.ch/chelsav2/EUR11/obs/annual/V2.1/bio01d/CHELSA_EUR11_obs_bio01d_2018_V.2.1.nc
	Temperature seasonality - CHELSA bio 04 = Temperature seasonality given by the standard deviation of mean monthly temperatures / 100 
	Download: https://os.zhdk.cloud.switch.ch/chelsav2/EUR11/obs/annual/V2.1/bio04d/CHELSA_EUR11_obs_bio04d_2018_V.2.1.nc
